# Code of Conduct

This repository expects technical, respectful, and evidence-based
collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Critique code, design, and evidence, not people.
- Keep discussions concrete, relevant, and reproducible.
- Assume good intent, but be direct about technical risk and correctness.
- Use private channels for security-sensitive topics.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- Doxxing, threats, or unwanted contact
- Spam, trolling, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Repeated bad-faith technical arguments after clear moderation

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of
conduct. Maintainers may also restrict repository interaction for repeated or
serious violations.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through a private channel.
